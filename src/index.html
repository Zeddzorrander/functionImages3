General Directions

In this part, you will once again enter a restriction on the range. However, this time, we will enter the interval using a center and a radius. Once you press the Submit button, and interval will appear on the x-axis. Move the points A and B to change this interval. Remember, the point B must always be to the right of point A or else you will not have a valid interval. The New Range button will allow you to enter a different subset of the range.
